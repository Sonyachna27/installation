About our wonderful team

InstallationPro has been providing local handyman services to homeowners, commercial organizations, real estate developers and third parties for last three years. We are located in Irvine, Orange County and cover whole Orange County and some close LA County cities (Torrance, Long Beach and others).

Our team is ready to help you with either small projects (shelf installation or TV mounting) or big projects (drywall repair or wood replacement). Most of the time we offer a “same day” service for our customers and ready to do a FREE virtual or physical consultation and estimation.

Our dispatch department will be happy to answer all questions and schedule a visit.

We provide high quality service at fair prices and respect the needs of each client and try to solve any problem as soon as possible. We would love to help you!
